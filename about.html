About the Group

Built in Jamaica.
Built for the world.

Who we are

One group.
Many futures.

Nexxcentric Group is a Jamaican holding company building a portfolio of brands across the categories that shape how people look, live, and grow.

Each company under the Group is built with the same standard — strategy first, quality non-negotiable, and a long view on what the brand can become. From digital services to retail to fashion to beauty, the portfolio is intentional.

Our standard

—

Strategy before execution

Every company in the portfolio is built with a clear rationale — not a trend, not a reaction.

—

Caribbean first, globally ready

Each brand is built to serve the region it knows best while operating at a global standard.

—

Quality is non-negotiable

Across every vertical, the output reflects a commitment to doing the work properly.

—

Long-term thinking

We build for what the brand becomes — not just what it needs today.

What we stand for

The principles
behind the portfolio.

01

Diagnose before prescribing

We don't recommend before we understand. Every engagement starts with honest assessment — not a predetermined solution.

02

Caribbean excellence

Built in Jamaica, operating at a global standard. The region has always had the talent. We're building the infrastructure to match.

03

Built to last

Every brand in the portfolio is built for longevity — not a moment, not a trend. Strategy, identity, and execution that compounds over time.

04

Human creativity first

Technology is a tool. Strategy and creativity are the work. Every company in the group is led by human thinking, not automated shortcuts.

05

One standard, every brand

Across all four companies — strategy, quality, and execution meet the same bar. The name on the door changes. The standard doesn't.

06

Equity through access

Premium positioning doesn't mean inaccessible. Each brand is built to serve its market with the quality it deserves — at every price point.
